I ran a Half Marathon in January!

To everyone who does not believe me, this page confirms that I ran 21.19 km in c. 2.5 hours on 19 January 2020.

As seen above I made my way from the beautiful city centre of Frankfurt to the ugly city centre of Offenbach and back. Outlined below, you can find some interesting and some less interesting stats about my workout. Thanks to the amazing technology that exists today, we can find that i burned more than 2,000 kilocalories during these 2.5 hours.

To everyone that has not yet tried to run a half or even a whole marathon, I can only recommend you to do so! It's a great personal achievement!
