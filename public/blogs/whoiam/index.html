Who I am

My name is Marius Constantin Dippe and I am 22 years old. I am born and raised in the beautiful city of Frankfurt, right in the center of Germany and Europe.

Since I was a child, I have always been interested in football. Apart from being a supporter of Eintracht Frankfurt, I spent the majority of my youth playing football myself. Even though I ended up studying finance, a professional career in football was an option for me after I graduated with my Abitur. Between the ages of 14 and 18, I was priviliged to play for youth teams of Eintracht Frankfurt, TSG Hoffenheim and several representative teams of the German Football Association (DFB).

Aside of sports and football, I started reading about wine and arts.
